Dr. Christian Conte is a licensed professional counselor, a certified Domestic Violence Counselor, and a Level V Anger Management Specialist, the highest certification from the National Anger Management Association. He has dedicated his career to helping individuals transform their lives through practical strategies and compassionate guidance. He is also the co-host of USA Network’s The Secret Life of Kids and a frequent guest on national and local programs such as Good Day, ESPN Radio, and CBS’s KDKA, sharing insights on emotional intelligence, anger management, and conflict resolution. His Yield Theory™ training has achieved significant success across multiple settings, including rehabilitation programs for violent offenders, family therapy, and professional athletics. Through his work, Dr. Conte helps people develop emotional control, improve communication, and foster healthier personal and professional relationships.
